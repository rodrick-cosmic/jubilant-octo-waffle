What is accessibility, who does it affect and why does it matter?

Okay, but why should *you* care? Picture this, you watch a movie in a foreign language, one that has no captions and no scenes spoken in your native tongue, chances are by the end you will walk out of the cinema knowing nothing. However, subtitles help translate and help us understand what characters are saying and what is going on.
