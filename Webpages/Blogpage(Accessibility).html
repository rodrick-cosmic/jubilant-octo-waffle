What is accessibility, who does it affect and why does it matter?

What is accessibility, and why does it matter. Before I answer this, I’d like to conduct something of an experiment, on my mark close your eyes-now and try read this next sentence. Without accessibility people who have sight impairments are not able to engage or interact with many facets of everyday life, even now unless you have super vision, or didn’t close your eyes it would have been impossible to read this far. In a digital space there is a lot we as designers can do to facilitate and even accommodate people with disabilities so that they may share the same experiences as us regardless of any impairments. This is where accessibility comes in, designing with inclusivity in mind. Accessibility refers to measures that allow any and all people to perceive, understand, navigate, and interact with a medium or their environment.
