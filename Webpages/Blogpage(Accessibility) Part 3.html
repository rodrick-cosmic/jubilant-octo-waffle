What is accessibility, who does it affect and why does it matter?

Okay great but what exactly is accessibility???? Accessibility refers to facilitating people with disabilities AND allowing them to actively participate in our society. Now what’s important to understand is that this does not only relate to digital spaces or even real life ones, instead, it is multi-faceted. Just how there are audio cues for the blind, there are similarly display options for video games like Call Of Duty. https://www.pcgamer.com/call-of-duty-black-ops-cold-war-colorblind-options/ (Links to an external site.) The gaming space has actually improved and made many options for accessibility, of these is the colorblind options mentioned for Call Of Duty, however, as recent as 2020, Naughty Dog’s The Last Of Us Part II, was praised for its multitude of accessibility options. “60+ settings make this Naughty Dog's most accessible game yet” (Gallant, 2020)
